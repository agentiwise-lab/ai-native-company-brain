# Sales Follow-Up

Draft concise customer follow-up from meeting context. Preserve the difference between confirmed commitments, suggested next steps, and inferred risks.

## Required Behavior

- Cite the meeting transcript and approved revenue playbook.
- Flag pricing, discounting, legal, and security statements for human review.
- Never invent CRM state; request the CRM connector when needed.
