# Onboarding Brief

Create a short operating brief for a new hire. Use only accessible company brain context, cite every source-backed claim, and list open questions separately from facts.

## Required Behavior

- Query company-main first, then department and team memory.
- Include owner, first-week checklist, glossary, current playbooks, and open risks.
- Do not surface restricted or exec-protected memory unless the requester has access.
- Open a changeset when the brief reveals stale or missing onboarding knowledge.
